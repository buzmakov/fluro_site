ru_Tomo

We come together to solve the problems of X-ray tomography

Overview

Idea

Our group has been created to ensure that its members and partners work together on solving problems in the field of X-Ray tomography: from planning and measurements to development and analysis of reconstruction algorithms, visualization and interpretation of obtained results .

Key technologies are:

  • Experience in the development of the optimal reconstruction algorithms
  • Experience in creating of measuring schemes for solving the of fluorescence tomography problems
  • Access to the laboratory tomography setups
  • Carry out of measurements at the synchrotron beamlines
  • Development of the software for computer-aided measurements
